My Story

How I got into Design
  1 Minute Read  

My rendezvous with design happened back in 2012 when I was completing my undergrad in CSE. I decided to take up the task of designing my parents' house back in India. I devoted two years of my life designing every nook and corner of this house, from the layouts to most of the customized furniture pieces. The process made me fall in love with Design. I had found my passion.

A year after joining Myntra as a software engineer, I quit my job to venture into the domain of design because I simply couldn't resist it. I tried to find good programs for furniture design in India but as luck had it, I couldn't find any program that met my requirements. Meanwhile, I took up an internship with an interior designer thinking that could help me get my foot into the door but that didn't work out as I had expected.

Next, I decided to try my own design venture. I set up a small team of carpenters, painters, and upholsterers and I designed furniture pieces to sell online. While designing the website for this venture, I quickly realized what I was missing in this entire process.

| There it was, my aha moment!

I was missing the power of technology. As much as I fell in love with design, my understanding of technology was a powerful tool that gave me the ability to have a much bigger impact. It was an invaluable skill which I couldn't let go. Here, I decided to work at the cusp of Design and Technology — right at the sweet spot because that's where I could create something meaningful and truly serve my purpose.

And I think you know what happened next :)
