High On Graphs

This project aimed at solving the problem of visualizing and querying large graph datasets.

After experimenting with existing community detection techniques like SNAP, METIS and iGraph, we came up with a Community Detection Algorithm based on influencial nodes of the graph to hierarchically cluster it.
