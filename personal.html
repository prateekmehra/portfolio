Parichay. Life.

Posted by Prateek Mehra on Sunday, 10 June 2012

I compiled this short memoire for my students at Parichay, an NGO in Delhi, with photos and videos taken over my teaching tenure there, using my phone/digital camera to leave with them everlasting memories of our time together.
