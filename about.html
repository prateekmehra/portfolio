My Story

How I got into Design
1 minute read

My rendevous with design happened back in 2012 when I was completing my undergrad in CSE. I decided to take up the project of designing my parents' house back in India. I devoted two years of my life designing every nook and corner of this house, from the layouts to most of the customized furniture pieces. The process made me fall in love with Design. I had found my passion.

A year after joining Myntra as a Software Engineer, I quit my job to venture into the domain of design because I simply couldn't resist it. I tried to find good programs for furniture design in India but as luck had it, I couldn't find any programs that matched my requirements. Meanwhile, I took up an internship with an interior designer thinking that could help me get my foot into the door but that didn't work out as I had expected.

Next, I decided to try my own furniture design venture. I setup a small team of carpenters, painters and upholsterers and designed and manufactured some furniture pieces to sell online. While designing the website for this venture, I quickly realised what I was missing in this entire process.

| There it was, my aha moment!

I was missing technology, my beautiful code. As much as I fell in love with design, my life's work had been in technology and I had loved it as much as my love for design till this very moment. Here, I decided I wanted to be working at the cusp of Design and Technology, right at the sweet spot.

And I think you know what happened next :)

PS: If you want to see what really got me into design, I have it compiled into a book for you to flip through right under this note. Go ahead, get a peek!
